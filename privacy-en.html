Audio Converter Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 19, 2026

Welcome to Audio Converter. We are fully committed to protecting your privacy and personal information. This policy explains how we collect, use, store and protect your information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

Audio Files: The core function of this app is audio format conversion. Your audio files are processed only locally on your device (or on a strictly encrypted temporary server and deleted immediately after conversion). We will never back up, collect, store or share your audio content with any third party.

Device & Usage Data: To improve app performance and fix crashes, we may collect anonymous device model, OS version and basic crash logs. This data does not include any personally identifiable information.

Payment & Subscription Info: All payments for VIP services (monthly, yearly, lifetime) are processed directly by Apple (App Store). We do NOT collect, access or store sensitive payment information such as credit card details or Apple ID passwords. We only receive anonymous purchase verification from Apple to activate your VIP access.

2. How We Use Your Information

To provide core audio conversion services.

To verify your VIP membership and manage subscription status.

To analyze app performance, fix bugs and improve user experience.

3. Information Sharing & Disclosure

We solemnly promise that we will never sell, rent or trade your personal information to any third party. We will not disclose your information except as required by law or by judicial authorities.

4. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, use, modification, damage or loss.

5. Your Rights

You can uninstall the app to delete all local data at any time. You can also manage or cancel auto-renewable subscriptions in iOS Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions.
